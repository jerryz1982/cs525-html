Frequently Asked Questions

How does PiGuard differentiate from other home security product based on Raspberry Pi?
PiGuard uses Raspberry Pi and various sensors to collect alarms and metrics. However, PiGuard is not just another home security robot on Raspberry Pi. We also provide a cloud-based smart home management solution to let you control your PiGuard agents at home from anywhere on any device.

Is there a contract for the service?
No. All you need is to buy the hardware from us and do the activation. Once the device is activated, you will be able to manage the device and watch the security events of your home on our cloud dashboard. It is essentially one time spending.

Is my data secure?
PiGuard employs encryption technologies to keep control and data plane from being compromised by hackers.
