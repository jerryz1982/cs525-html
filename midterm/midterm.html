This is a heart shaped island. I got to this island because my ship sinked and I was sent to this island by the waves.

Knife

A knife can be versatile because with it I can do a lot of things including hunting animals, cutting food and self protection.

Lighter

A lighter can light up fire, which keeps me warm and can cook food. Moreover, boiling sea water can create fresh water with its vapor.

Book

A book can keep me from getting bored and tired of life. If I happen to take Robinson Crusoe, that would be great.

The Island

Where is the island?
Somewhere in the Pacific.
How to Survive?
Use knife to cut some wood and use lighter to make fire. Do some hunting and cherry picking. Make fresh water with fire and sea water.
